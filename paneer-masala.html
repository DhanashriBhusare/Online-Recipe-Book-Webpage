Paneer Masala Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer (cubed)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 Onions (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 cup Cream
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Stir in tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Add red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Pour in cream and sprinkle garam masala. Simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
